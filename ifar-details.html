The IFAR Consortium is a network of Indonesian university partners launched by ISRSF in December 2021. It consists of member universities that have established an Institute for Advanced Research (IFAR) on their campus to help achieve the highest standards of research, publications, and teaching in the social sciences and public policy. ISRSF supports IFAR Consortium members with direct funding and through new PhDs trained through the Arryman Scholars Initiative at Northwestern University in the U.S. and at SOAS University of London in the U.K. ISRSF works closely with the Consortium to help members establish and maintain strong intellectual habitats, pursue research and teaching excellence, and provide a model of innovation for other universities across Indonesia.
